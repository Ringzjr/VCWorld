1. Manhattan Distance: 
    Sheer distance from a state's position to the nearest dirt.

2. Manhattan Distance (accumulative):
    Distance from a state's position to the nearest dirt,
    + the distance from that nearest dirt, to the next nearest dirt
    + etc, until no more dirt. 
    
3. Snake Distance:
    Length of shortest non-disconnected path to nearest dirt. 
    Like Manhattan distance but considering walls.
